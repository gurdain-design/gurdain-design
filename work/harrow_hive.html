Harrow Hive Badge & Branding

Harrow Hive Football Club is a Sunday League football team that established in 2019. The goal behind creating the badge was to ensure there was a modern feel and uniqueness to the badge. The club branding requires a classy and modern feel. In addition to creating the graphics I have been the club photographer and have also created animation style visuals.
