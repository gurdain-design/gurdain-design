Shergz / Logo & Branding

Shergz is a gaming YouTuber and Twitch Streamer. The goal here was to create a strong bold logo that was not too serious and playful. The colour palette required shades of purple. Additionally assets were created for YouTube and Twitch. The offline screen for Twitch follows a simple design with a polygraphic background which is used throughout the branding. The intro for YouTube videos follows a reveal of the logo as if an old screen is turning on. To view Shergz' content on the two platforms click the following links - YouTube / Twitch

Harrow Hive Football Club / Badge & Branding

Harrow Hive Football Club is a Sunday League football team that established in 2019. The goal behind creating the badge was to ensure there was a modern feel and uniqueness to the badge. There was great care taken to balance the club colours - dark blue and yellow - to ensure one was not overpowering the other. The club branding requires a classy and modern feel.
